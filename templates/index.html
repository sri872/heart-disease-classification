 Clinical Ethics & System Limitations

                

                    

                        
Purpose & Intent

                        
Pulse AI is designed as a Clinical Decision Support System (CDSS) to assist medical professionals. It is not a replacement for independent clinical judgment or specialized diagnostic imaging.

                    

                    

                        
Data Integrity

                        
Trained on the UCI Heart Disease cohort augmented with GAN-synthetic data. Results may vary across demographics not fully represented in the training set.

                    

                    

                        
Regulatory Status

                        
Prototype for academic demonstration. Requires clinical trials and regulatory approval (e.g., FDA/CE) before real-world hospital deployment.

                    

                

            

        


        

            
            

                

                    
                    
Core Engine

                    
Powered by a specialized Random Forest ensemble refined on a mixed 2.3k record cohort for maximum predictive stability.

                

                

                    
                    
Explainable AI

                    
SHAP (SHapley Additive exPlanations) values provide individual-level clinical transparency for every diagnostic result.

                

                

                    
                    
GAN Resilience

                    
Augmented with high-fidelity synthetic data via Generative Adversarial Networks to ensure model robustness and privacy.
